Description

The space

This place is your home away from home. It has the feel of a resort and comfort of a home. Room can easily fit 3 adults. Extra mattress is provided. Fully equipped kitchen cooking plates, microwave, fridge and necessary utensils. Linen and towels are also available at no extra cost, extra facilities include a cleaner whose number will be provided to guest and who will clean the apt as per your convenience and requirement for a minimum daily fee.

Guest access

Guest have plenty of Parking Space and Access to the Swimming Pool. There is also a small Gymnasium overlooking the fields.

Interaction with guests

I will be happy to answer any of your queries and provide you with links/information for various bookings like car rental, restaurants, river cruises, Yacht rental, night clubs, etc
A local contact number will provided on booking for any issues faced during your stay or any other assistance required.

The neighborhood

Glimpse of our surrounding neighborhood;-The landmark of Siolim, St Anthonys Church which is over 500years old and the famous Siolim market, be sure to catch the Wednesday markets if your looking for a fresh catch followed by a home cooked meal. Apartment is located within close proximity to good supermarkets shelving almost everything and anything one would require when on holiday, pharmacies, quaint cafes, restaurants with WiFi and general provision stores are also in vicinity.
